Defend the King's Tea

Yo

The colonists have come to seize your tea and throw it into the Crab nebula. As the last redcoat standing remaining on the intergalactic HMS Clipper, you are armed with a powerful laser.

    Keep the following tips in mind:
  • Leaving the ship will deprive you of Oxygen and impact your health -- so do this sparingly
  • Use your laser's greater range to your advantage. There is no need to aim-- the laser is equipped with auto-aim technology.
  • Your health will regen slowly on its own
  • If you can eliminate 100 colonists, the rest should turn tail and flee.
  • Good luck!
