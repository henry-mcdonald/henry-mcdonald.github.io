    Keep the following tips in mind:
  1. Teleport around the map by clicking once every few seconds -- your teleportation recharge is located at the top-left and colored yellow
  2. Use your laser's greater range to your advantage. There is no need to aim-- the laser is equipped with auto-aim technology.

  4. If you can eliminate 100 enemies, you will win.
  5. Your health will regen slowly on its own
  6. Good luck!
